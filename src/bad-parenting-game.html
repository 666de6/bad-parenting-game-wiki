Bad Parenting 1: Mr. Red Face

Bad Parenting 1: Mr. Red Face is a Sad horror Game. Mr. Red Face, a fictional character created by adults to teach their children. It's said that he often appears late at night to give gifts to well-behaved kids. But is the story of Mr. Red Face really that simple?
